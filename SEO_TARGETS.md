# MedMaths SEO Targets

This build focuses MedMaths around medication maths rather than broad diagnostic calculators.

## Core ranking targets

1. mg to mL calculator for medicine
2. tablet dosage calculator
3. IV drip rate calculator / mL/hr to gtt/min
4. drops/min to mL/hr calculator
5. infusion time calculator
6. mg/kg to mL calculator
7. units to mL calculator
8. C1V1=C2V2 dilution calculator
9. reconstitution calculator
10. vial dose to mL calculator
11. BSA calculator for dosing
12. ideal body weight calculator
13. creatinine clearance calculator Cockcroft-Gault
14. mg/kg to tablets calculator

## SEO rules for future pages

- One calculator page should target one calculation intent.
- Put the calculator above long educational content.
- Show formula, units, worked examples, safety scope, references, and related calculators.
- Avoid broad clinical score pages unless they directly support medication dosing.
- Do not create thin pages for every numeric long-tail query. Add examples inside the main calculator first.
- Keep canonical, title, meta description, sitemap, and registry aligned.
